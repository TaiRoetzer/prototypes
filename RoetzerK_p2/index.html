MUSIC INSPIRES

September 1, 2, 3 2023

Downtown Oak Harbor, WA on SE Pioneer Way

The festival is dedicated to producing a world-class, 3-day musical event that sparks joy while building a sense of community, that brings our island neighbors together while establishing lasting connections with visitors.

The festival aims to add vibrancy to downtown Oak Harbor while generating revenue for Whidbey Island business, and that provides equal-access music education opportunities with scholarships and instrument programs that encourage local young people to express themselves through music.

OUR MISSION

Building Community One Note
at a Time

For the past eleven years the Oak Harbor Music Festival has provided a weekend of free musical entertainment in the City of Oak Harbor, on beautiful Whidbey Island.

The festival has provided a total of over $60,000 in scholarships to dozens of Whidbey Island graduating seniors who plan to study music or build their performance skills in college.
